I'm Kyle, the artisan behind the guitars, and I've dedicated myself to crafting fine musical instruments.

A Focus on Design and Quality

At Cosmic Vibrations, I believe that every instrument tells a story. Each guitar that leaves my workshop is not just a refined tool for musical expression, but also piece of art in itself.

I pour my soul into every step of the process, from selecting the finest materials to the final tuning. Each instrument is meticulously crafted to inspire and elevate your musical journey. While I nod to artisan Luthier techniques and incoporate tradition in many aspects of the process, I also embrace the precision of modern CNC and CAD design.
